About Us

Our Mission

Our mission at Interior 2000 is to provide quality work in a timely and efficient manner. We aim to please and do our best to work with the customers schedule. We believe that our honesty and knowledge of the trade are second to none.

What we do

Formed in 1999, Interior 2000 has been providing our customers with quality products and unmatched service for over a decade. We provide homeowners, residential builders, and commercial contractors with many wonderful products and services, such as frameless & custom shower enclosures, closets and storage systems, custom mirrors and glass, gas fireplaces and logsets, and even complete bathroom renovation.

What sets us apart?

Our attention to detail and in house employees are what sets us apart from others. We offer competitive pricing and pass along our savings to our clients! All of our projects are personally overseen by owner David Dowdy or project manager Joey Dowdy to provide the utmost quality. Please browse our website and view some of the many products we offer, then give us a call to schedule a free in home consultation, or to visit our showroom in Powhatan. We’re fully licensed and insured, and accept all major credit cards! We look forward to working with you!

Our Vision

Our vision is to continue to provide quality service. Interior 2000 undergoes constant training and expos to keep up with the ever-changing products and trends.
