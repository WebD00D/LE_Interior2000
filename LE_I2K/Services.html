Our Services

Check out our list of services below. If you have any questions, feel free to give us a shout!



Shower Enclosures

We measure, fabricate, and install an array of shower doors. Everything from your most basic framed style unit all the way to the most complex heavy glass frameless.

Gas Fireplaces and Logs

We supply, install and service direct vent, vent free, and wood burning fireplace inserts. We also sell, install, and service gas logsets vented and non vented. We carry many brands such as Lennox, FMI, Majestic, and R.H. Peterson.

Custom Mirrors and Glass

We can custom cut and install any size or shape mirror or glass, including bevelled mirrors, framed mirrors, and glass shelves for bars or cabinets.

Closets and Storage Systems

We measure, cut, and install all types of closet and garage shelving. Everything from ventilated wire, wood, and melamine.

Bath and Door Hardware

We install a variety of bath and door hardware that inlcude paper holders, towel bars, towel rings, robe hooks, grab bars, door knobs, levers, deadbolts, as well as handle sets for interior and exterior doors.

Complete Bathroom Renovations

Interior 2000 has also performed many complete bathroom renovations. Anything from cabinetry, tile, cultured marble, plumbing, lighting, etc. Many references available.
